Frequently Asked Questions

What type of audio drivers do these headphones use?

The Armor Version.H1 is equipped with 50mm Neodymium drivers.

How does the RGB Light Effect work?

The RGB Light Effect Button allows you to customize the lighting on your headphones for a dynamic gaming experience. Single press to cycle through different RGB colors. Long press to turn the lights off. Single press again to turn the lights back on. Double Press to automatically loop through all RGB lighting modes.

What type of microphone does this headset have?

It comes with a detachable noise-cancelling microphone designed to reduce background noise.

What is the connectivity type of these headphones?

The Armor Version.H1 features a USB Type-C connector for seamless, high-speed audio transmission. Additionally, the package includes a USB-C to USB-A adapter, ensuring compatibility with a wider range of devices

Are the ear cushions replaceable?

Yes, the ear cushions are replaceable.
